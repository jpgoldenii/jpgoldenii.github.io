Target Audience

A description of the target audience is listed below:

  • Median age - 44
  • Median household income - $50,576
  • Some college education - 45%
  • Families, married - 68%
  • English speaking - 98%
  • Access of site: 57.3% mobile device, 48.7% desktop computer
